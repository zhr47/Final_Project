Welcome to our Airbnb price predictor

This is a machine learning model, to help owners predict a price for their property depending on the location.

The model is only available for the city of Amseterdam. This project uses several listing features to try and predict price such as, accommodates, bedrooms, bathrooms... To model the relationship between rental prices and listing features, we used multiple regression. Our coefficient of multiple determination for multiple regression(R-Squared) is 35%.

Eat

Amsterdam has many treats to eat while you visit. Bitterballen, pictured above, are crispy meatballs are a go to snack. The almost inevitable to come across the wonderful treat, as it is one of the most popular bar foods. The recipe is fairly straightforward, it is mainly composed of slow cooked meat, which is cooled till firm then rolled into balls, breaded and deep fried. Served with spicy mustard it's a treat that can't be beat.

Pray

The windmills in Holland are an amazing sight to see while visiting Amsterdam, historically they have served many purposes. However their most important function is pumping water out of the lowlands and back into the flowing rivers. Today's windmills have been restored to once again grind grain and to teach the old Dutch culture to visitors. A fantastic way to see these mills are by bicycle, so grab your helmets and head to any tourist office for route to view the most beautiful mills.
